The outermost layer, like a shell, interacts with the tangible world, offering protection, structure, and practicality. There are many types of Shell Archetypes, and it is the most common archetype as well.
#### Architect’s Soul
- **Theme**: Building, Creation of inanimate things
- **Abilities**: Creating tools, buildings, bridges, etc.
#### Shaper's Soul
- **Theme**: Transformation and refinement.
- **Abilities**: Altering forms, improving objects, or refining raw materials into perfected states.
#### Scribe's Soul
- **Theme**: Knowledge and memory.
- **Abilities**: Recording events, preserving information, or recalling precise details.
#### Guardian's Soul
- **Theme**: Protection and defense.
- **Abilities**: Creating shields, reinforcing structures, or intercepting harmful forces.
- Notable Characters: [[CHARACTERS/Naediia/index|Naediia]]
#### Harvester’s Soul
- **Theme**: Collection and absorption.
- **Abilities**: Drawing energy from surroundings, gathering resources, or storing power.
#### Crafter's Soul
 - **Theme**: Practicality and ingenuity.
 - **Abilities**: Repairing broken objects, crafting functional tools, or improving pre-existing designs.
#### Forager's Soul
- **Theme**: Survival and resourcefulness.
- **Abilities**: Locating natural resources, identifying useful materials, or harvesting safely.
#### Tinkerer's Soul
- **Theme**: Mechanization and precision.
- **Abilities**: Constructing intricate mechanisms, fine-tuning devices, or creating small automated constructs.
#### Planter's Soul
- **Theme**: Cultivation and growth.
- **Abilities**: Encouraging plants to grow, restoring damaged ecosystems, or creating gardens.
#### Tidecaller's Soul
- **Theme**: Flow and motion.
- **Abilities**: Manipulating liquids, creating currents, or ensuring smooth transitions.
- Notable Characters: Canelé
