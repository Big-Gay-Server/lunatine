The act of creating a [[CONCEPTS/Soul/index|Soul]] or [[CONCEPTS/Essence/index|Essence]] out of concentrated [[CONCEPTS/Dust/index|Dust]]. This is an ability that is unique to the [[SPECIES/Inna/index|Inna]].

[[SPECIES/Selhae/index|Selhae]] can use the power of [[CONCEPTS/Construction/index|Construction]], which is related to creation in that the user takes a soulpiece from their own soul and combines it with essence to make a [[SPECIES/Construct/index|Construct]], however these are not complete souls being created and thus is a construction rather than a creation.
