Lunatine is a worldbuilding project by LunarConstruct.

It started out as a quick little bit of lore for [[CHARACTERS/Merisdae]] back in 2016. I had just rebranded my UTAU voicebank from "Tsuki Hikari" to "Merisdae", and wanted to give her some backstory!

Soon, the world grew and grew, and completely changed many times over. Slowly it has morphed into the world it is today :)

Thank you for being interested in Lunatine!! It really means the world!
