title: Kohoii Solei War
---
1. an ongoing war between the settlement of [[LOCATIONS/Kohoii/index|Kohoii]] and the kingdom of [[LOCATIONS/Solei/index|Solei]], which began when Solei attempted to annex the neighboring settlement of Kohoii. 
2. Solei, being a much larger force than Kohoii, began by occupying the settlement. Kohoii’s mages and warriors tried to fight them off for a while, but a devastating battle claimed many casualties (including [[CHARACTERS/Naediia/index|Naediia]], the elder’s daughter and head mage) and left the people of Kohoii with little choice but to accept the occupation, for now.
    1. Solei used [[CHARACTERS/Kulith/index|Kulith]]’s powerful abilities as a weapon in this battle.
