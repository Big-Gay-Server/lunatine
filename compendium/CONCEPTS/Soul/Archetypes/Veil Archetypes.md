The middle layer acts as a veil or bridge between the surface and the core, with abilities focused on connection, discovery, and interaction. Veil Archetypes are slightly less common than Shell Archetypes, but are not unheard of in regular individuals.
#### Healer’s Soul
- **Theme**: Restoration and preservation.
- **Abilities**: Healing physical wounds, mending objects, or restoring emotional balance.
#### Seeker's Soul
- **Theme**: Discovery and exploration.
- **Abilities**: Sensing hidden truths, tracking, navigating, teleportation.
- Notable Characters: [[CHARACTERS/Esela/index|Esela]], [[CHARACTERS/Reika/index|Reika]]
#### Warden’s Soul
- **Theme**: Restraint and containment.
- **Abilities**: Binding forces, imprisoning energy, or restricting movement.
- Notable Characters : [[CHARACTERS/Merisdae/BuroMerisdae|BuroMerisdae]]
#### Shadow's Soul
- **Theme**: Subtlety and concealment.
- **Abilities**: Hiding, cloaking objects, or moving unseen.
- Notable Characters: [[CHARACTERS/Merisdae/FolMerisdae|FolMerisdae]]
#### Muse's Soul
- **Theme**: Inspiration and creativity.
- **Abilities**: Sparking innovation, influencing emotions, or enhancing artistic expression.
- Notable Characters: [[HiitoMerisdae]]
